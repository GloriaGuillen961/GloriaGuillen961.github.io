History

This Vineyard, set in the central Bourdeux region, was first created in 1812 during the reign of Tyrion of House Lannister in honor of his friend, Ser Bronn of the Blackwater. Originally, it served many purposes, it housed travelers along the Kingsroad, it served as the local spot for many Dothraki weddings (We are not haunted by the way, we had the Winchesters check a few years ago), and after served as an escape route for Tyrion when we all realized Cersei was crazy. Recently, it is still in the Lannister possession, and the wine made here is prize-winning.
